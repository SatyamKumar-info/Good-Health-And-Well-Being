Yoga & Exercise Guide

Stay Fit, Stay Healthy

🌞 Morning Yoga Routine

Start your day with a peaceful and energizing yoga session. Focus on breathing and stretching to awaken your body and mind.

  • Sun Salutation (Surya Namaskar) – 5 rounds
  • Cat-Cow Stretch – 10 reps
  • Downward Dog – Hold for 30 seconds
  • Child's Pose – 1 minute

🏃‍♀️ Cardio Exercises

Cardio workouts help improve your heart health, stamina, and burn fat effectively. These can be done indoors or outdoors.

  • Jumping jacks – 2 mins
  • High knees – 1 min
  • Running or Brisk walking – 15-30 mins
  • Burpees – 10 reps x 3 sets

🧘‍♀️ Flexibility & Balance

Enhance your balance and prevent injury with these yoga poses and stretches that promote flexibility.

  • Tree Pose – 30 seconds each leg
  • Triangle Pose – 30 seconds each side
  • Seated Forward Bend – 1 minute
  • Bridge Pose – 30 seconds hold

🍃 Relaxation & Recovery

Don't forget to cool down and relax. Recovery is essential for muscle repair and mental peace.

  • Savasana – 5 minutes
  • Deep breathing (Pranayama) – 5-10 minutes
  • Meditation or Guided Visualization
